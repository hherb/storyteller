# Getting Started with Storyteller

Welcome to Storyteller! This guide will help you create your first illustrated storybook.

## What You'll Need

- A Mac with Apple Silicon (M1, M2, M3, or M4 chip)
- At least 32GB of RAM
- About 50GB of free disk space (for AI models)
- macOS Ventura (13.0) or later

## Installation

*Installation instructions will be added once the application is ready for release.*

## Creating Your First Story

### Step 1: Start a New Story

When you open Storyteller, you'll see the option to create a new story. Click "New Story" to begin.

### Step 2: Answer the Story Guide's Questions

Storyteller will ask you questions to help shape your story:

- **Who is the main character?** (A child, an animal, a magical creature)
- **Where does the story take place?** (A forest, a city, under the sea)
- **What happens in the story?** (An adventure, learning something new, making a friend)

Don't worry about having all the answers - the guide will help you develop your ideas!

### Step 3: Write Your Pages

For each page, you'll:
1. Write or refine the text with AI assistance
2. Generate an illustration that matches your story
3. Choose from different illustration styles

### Step 4: Export Your Book

When you're happy with your story, export it as a PDF ready for printing!

## Tips for Great Stories

- **Keep it simple**: Young children love stories with clear, simple plots
- **Use repetition**: Repeating phrases help children engage with the story
- **Include emotions**: Name feelings to help children understand them
- **End positively**: Leave young readers with a happy, satisfied feeling

## Getting Help

If you need help while using Storyteller, look for the "?" icon in the app for contextual tips.
